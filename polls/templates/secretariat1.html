Secretariat

A message from the Secretariat
The task of defining an MUN is a futile endeavor that limits the true depth of the experience. However, if one were to describe a MUN as a journey, it would be one of active exploration, invigoration and ideation.
It is, at its core, a healthy and extremely energizing activity with enormous potential for developing and further enhancing leadership, reflection, creativity, and critical thinking and teamwork abilities.

MISMUN's goal is to assist young minds in exploring this journey, traversing new ground, honing their skills and most importantly, stretching their mental faculties in new directions. The Secretariat has put its best foot forward for the eleventh edition of MISMUN to help every participant navigate this wonderful journey and deliver a memorable, inspirational and educational experience.
